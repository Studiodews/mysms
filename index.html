Please read the instructions below to start using Mysms : 

1. Goto BETASMS.COM
create an account and purchase an sms CREDIT.
2. Then open Mysms Software Application and insert your password and username/email address which you generated from BETASMS.COM.
3. Put unlimited mobile phone numbers you desire in a country prefix e.g 234xxxxx.
4.Put your desired name as the sender, note:- the maximum character to be on the sender section is eleven (11).
5.Write your messages in the text area box , be mindful of the lenght of text you input as the maxumum characters you can type in the text area box is (160), after composing your message just click on SEND.
